# Financial Risk & Cost Intelligence Platform

## Overview
This project simulates a banking analytics platform designed to analyse financial performance, identify cost leakages, and evaluate risk-adjusted outcomes across business units and portfolios.

The platform is inspired by enterprise banking analytics used in organisations such as Barclays and JP Morgan.

---

## Business Objectives
- Identify cost variances and leakages
- Monitor financial risk exposure
- Evaluate portfolio performance
- Support scenario-based decision-making
